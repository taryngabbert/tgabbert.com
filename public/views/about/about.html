about me.

I am a MEAN stack web developer based out of Dallas, TX.

I graduated from University of Texas at Dallas from the Davidson Management Honors Program in 2015 with a BS in Global Business and Finance. After graduation I worked for United Capital Financial Advisers, when I was put on a project that spurred my interest in web development. My interest turned into a passion, and I decided I had to know more. A year after starting in Finance, I made a giant leap and went to DevMountain, an intensive three month bootcamp, to learn all the ins-and-outs of how to build web applications.

Now, having graduated DevMountain, I am currently working as a mentor for their current student class. The class will be ending in late November, so I'm currently looking for my next adventure with a company that inspires me.

In my spare time you can find me curled up at a coffee shop with my kindle. Traveling to a new place, or shredding some powder on my snowboard. I enjoy trying new things, learning about new cultures, volunteering in my community and challening myself to become a better me.
